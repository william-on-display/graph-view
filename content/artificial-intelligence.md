# Artificial Intelligence

The study and engineering of intelligent systems. AI asks whether machines can think, learn, and reason — drawing on [[Computation]], [[Information Theory]], and [[Philosophy of Mind]].

Modern AI (deep learning, large language models) raises new questions about [[Consciousness]], [[Ethics]], and the nature of understanding itself.
