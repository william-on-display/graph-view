# Free Will

Do we have genuine choice, or are our actions determined by prior causes? The free will debate sits at the intersection of [[Philosophy of Mind]], [[Consciousness]], and physics.

If the universe is deterministic, free will may be an illusion. [[Quantum Mechanics]] introduces indeterminacy, but randomness isn't the same as freedom. The [[Law of Non-Contradiction]] constrains the debate: we cannot both have and lack free will in the same sense.
