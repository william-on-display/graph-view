# Rationalism

The view that reason is the primary source of knowledge. Some truths can be known independently of sensory experience — through intuition and deduction alone.

Descartes' [[Cogito]] is a paradigmatic rationalist move: arriving at certainty through pure thought. Rationalism stands in tension with [[Empiricism]], and both feed into [[Epistemology]].

Key rationalists include Descartes, Spinoza, and Leibniz.
