# Computation

The study of what can be computed and how efficiently. Turing machines, algorithms, complexity classes — computation formalizes the notion of an effective procedure.

Grounded in [[Mathematics]] and [[Logic]], shaped by [[Information Theory]]. Computation is central to [[Artificial Intelligence]] and connects to [[Philosophy of Mind]] through the computational theory of mind.
