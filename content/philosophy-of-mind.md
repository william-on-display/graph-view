# Philosophy of Mind

What is the relationship between mind and body? Philosophy of mind investigates the nature of mental states, [[Consciousness]], and their relation to the physical world.

Rooted in [[Epistemology]] and the [[Cogito]] — the certainty of one's own mental life. Connects to [[Artificial Intelligence]] through questions about machine consciousness and to [[Quantum Mechanics]] through observer-dependent interpretations.
