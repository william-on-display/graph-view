# Quantum Mechanics

The physics of the very small — atoms, photons, electrons. Quantum mechanics describes nature probabilistically and introduces phenomena like superposition, entanglement, and wave-particle duality.

Built on [[Mathematics]] and validated as one of the most precise [[Scientific Theories]] ever developed. Its interpretation raises deep questions for [[Philosophy of Mind]] and [[Consciousness]].
