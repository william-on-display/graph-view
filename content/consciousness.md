# Consciousness

The subjective quality of experience — "what it is like" to be something. The hard problem of consciousness asks why physical processes give rise to subjective experience at all.

Deeply connected to [[Philosophy of Mind]] and the [[Cogito]]. [[Evolution]] explains how brains arose, but not necessarily why they are conscious. [[Artificial Intelligence]] forces the question: can machines be conscious?
