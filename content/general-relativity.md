# General Relativity

Einstein's theory describing gravity as the curvature of spacetime caused by mass and energy. One of the pillars of modern physics alongside [[Quantum Mechanics]].

General relativity relies on [[Mathematics]] (differential geometry) and has been confirmed as a [[Scientific Theories|scientific theory]] through gravitational lensing, GPS corrections, and gravitational wave detection.
