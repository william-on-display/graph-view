# Language

Language is the medium of thought, communication, and knowledge. Philosophy of language asks how words refer, how meaning arises, and what the limits of linguistic expression are.

Closely tied to [[Logic]] (formal languages) and [[Epistemology]] (linguistic knowledge). Language is central to [[Computation]] and [[Artificial Intelligence]] — large language models operate entirely in the domain of language.
