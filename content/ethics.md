# Ethics

The study of right and wrong, good and bad. Ethics asks how we ought to live and what we owe to each other.

Requires [[Epistemology]] (how do we know what's right?), [[Logic]] (reasoning about moral claims), and [[Consciousness]] (moral patients must be sentient). Ethics is critical to [[Artificial Intelligence]] — how should we build systems that affect human lives?
