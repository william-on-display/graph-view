# Scientific Theories

A scientific theory is a well-substantiated explanation of some aspect of the natural world, based on repeated testing and confirmation through the [[Scientific Method]].

Theories must be falsifiable ([[Falsifiability]]) and are always provisional — subject to revision. Examples include [[Evolution]], [[General Relativity]], and [[Quantum Mechanics]].
