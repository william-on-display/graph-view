# Scientific Method

A systematic procedure for investigating phenomena: observe, hypothesize, experiment, analyze, conclude. The method combines [[Empiricism]] (observation) with [[Logic]] (reasoning) and is constrained by [[Falsifiability]].

The scientific method is the practical application of [[Philosophy of Science]] and produces [[Scientific Theories]].
